Supplementary Table 1: List of single-cell expression quantitative trait TR loci (sc-eTRs) (FDR < 5%)
The details for each column are: 
chromosome: chromosome of the single-cell eTR (hg38)
start coordinate (hg38): start coordinate of the single-cell eTR
end coordinate (hg38): end coordinate of the single-cell eTR
motif: motif of the single-cell eTR
cell_type: cell type of the single-cell eTR
eGene: eGene associated with the single-cell eTR
nominal p-value: nominal (uncorrected) p-value of the single-cell eTR
effect size: effect size of the single-cell eTR
standard error: standard error of the single-cell eTR
